Record a meeting from this laptop

Run this on the computer that's in the call. It captures the shared tab/window audio plus your microphone, and uploads the recording here when you stop — no server-side browser needed.

  1. Click Start recording.
  2. In the share dialog, pick the meeting tab (or the whole screen) and enable “Share tab audio” — without it, other participants won't be recorded.
  3. When the call ends, click Stop & upload. You'll get a link to the meeting.
